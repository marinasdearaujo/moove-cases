Lead Generation & Telemarketing

Automated Outreach and Follow-Up that Keeps a Pipeline Warm

Outbound was going out in bursts and dying in silence. First contact happened, follow-up depended on somebody remembering, and any lead who did not reply straight away was effectively lost. The pipeline looked busy at the top and empty everywhere else.

What we built

We built a prospecting flow that qualifies leads before anyone spends time on them, sends the first touch, and then runs structured follow-up on its own. Leads who go quiet are re-engaged automatically on a schedule instead of being written off, and anyone who shows interest is routed straight to booking rather than into a queue. Every interaction is written back to the CRM, so the next conversation starts with full context instead of from zero.

First response and follow-up are now consistent without manual effort, cold leads are pulled back into the pipeline automatically, and the team spends its time on conversations that are already qualified rather than on chasing.
