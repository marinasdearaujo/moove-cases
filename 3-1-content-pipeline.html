AI Content Pipeline: Turning Interviews into Ad Creative at Scale

A brand had hours of raw interview footage and no efficient way to turn it into advertising. Finding the moments worth using meant watching everything, and captioning and formatting clips by hand was far too slow to keep a paid campaign fed with fresh creative.

What we built

We built an AI-assisted pipeline that transcribes long-form interviews and mines them for the strongest, most persuasive soundbites, then maps each clip to a specific campaign angle so the creative has a job before it is cut. From there it auto-captions and formats the clips into vertical, ad-ready video. Alongside the pipeline we delivered a reusable soundbite library and written editing direction, so the in-house team can keep producing at the same standard without us in the loop.

Weeks of manual editing collapsed into a repeatable process, and footage that had been sitting unused became a library of ad-ready clips the brand can draw from for every campaign cycle.
