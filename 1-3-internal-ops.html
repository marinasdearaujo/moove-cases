Internal AI Agents that Run Order Entry, Finance and Reporting

A food business was running order registration, financial control and operational reporting by hand, spread across disconnected tools. It was slow and error-prone, and by the time a report existed the decision it was meant to inform had already been made from memory.

What we built

We designed and deployed internal AI agents and automated workflows to take over the routine. Orders come in and get registered correctly without manual re-entry, financial records stay current instead of being reconciled at month end, and the operational reports the team used to assemble by hand are generated on their own. The separate tools were connected into a single flow, with the data validated as it moves rather than corrected after the fact.

Repetitive, error-prone routines were replaced by automation that runs unattended, entry errors dropped, and the team gained real-time visibility. Operational data stopped being a monthly archaeology project and became the input for decisions on pricing, purchasing and commercial strategy.
