AI WhatsApp Agent that Books, Reschedules and Recovers No-Shows

Service businesses were losing customers in the gap between message and reply. Bookings were handled by hand, every message that arrived after hours was a sale at risk, and clients who went quiet were never followed up with. The owner was the bottleneck, answering the same questions between appointments.

What we built

We built a WhatsApp AI attendant that runs the whole conversation in the brand's voice. It answers in natural language, qualifies what the customer actually needs, quotes, checks live availability, books and reschedules directly on the calendar, and resolves scheduling conflicts in real time instead of double-booking. It upsells adjacent services where they make sense, and automatically re-engages clients who stopped replying. Behind it sits a dashboard showing conversations, bookings and revenue, so the business can see what the agent is producing rather than take it on faith.

The result is instant response day and night, appointments booked with no human effort, and silent clients recovered automatically. The owner stopped answering messages and started reading a dashboard.
