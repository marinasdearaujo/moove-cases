Custom Web CRM with Live-Synced Pipelines and Automated Follow-Up

Leads lived in scattered notes and spreadsheets. Two people were working the same pipeline from different devices with different versions of the truth, and nobody could say with confidence which deals were actually moving and which had gone cold weeks ago.

What we built

We built a custom web CRM with a separate pipeline per business line, syncing live across devices so everyone sees the same board at the same time. Each lead carries a stage, an owner and a next action, so nothing sits in limbo. Automated follow-up reminders fire on their own, and the CRM is wired into the outreach flow so every contact and reply attaches to the lead automatically. No one has to remember to log anything.

The result is one source of truth instead of three. Follow-up happens whether or not someone remembers it, and the pipeline can be read at a glance instead of reconstructed from notes at the end of the week.
