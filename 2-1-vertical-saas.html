Four Vertical AI Attendants Built as SaaS Products

Small service businesses lose money the same way regardless of the trade: slow replies, manual scheduling and no follow-up. Barbershops, dental clinics, aesthetic clinics and auto detailing shops all had the same underlying problem, but each needed an attendant that already spoke its language, not a generic bot to configure from scratch.

What we built

We designed and shipped four productized AI attendants, one per vertical. They share a common core and diverge where it matters: conversation flows, service catalog, pricing logic and booking rules are specific to each trade, so the agent understands what a client is asking for from the first message. Each product ships with calendar, payment and CRM integration, an admin dashboard, and an onboarding flow that gets a new business live quickly instead of requiring a custom build.

The outcome is a repeatable product rather than a series of one-off projects. A new client goes live in days instead of weeks, with an agent that already knows their business, and the same core improves for everyone whenever it is upgraded.
